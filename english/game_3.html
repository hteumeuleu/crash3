Gems - The story doesn't end there though. For an Access All Areas pass, Crash will need to pick up gems as well as crystals. To get the gems you need to smash all the boxes and eat a serious number of apples (well above the RDA). On some levels, collecting gems will open up hidden areas and aid Crash's progression later in the game. There are six different colours of gem: clear, red, yellow, purple, blue and green, and on many levels Crash won't have the moves to get all of them first time around.
