Some of the effects on Crash 3 look very impressive. Take the water, for example. . . how did you make it look so. . . wet?
Obviously, the water hasn't been done on PlayStation before, people thought that there was no way you could get away from the obviously polygonal feel of a water surface on PlayStation. We tried to create a completely fluid feel on the jet-ski levels. The surface on the water is an environment map that reflects the sky. So you will notice on the third jet-ski level there's a volcano and the sky is very black and red. The water then looks like an oil slick with the red reflections on it. . . so it is an actual environment map.
