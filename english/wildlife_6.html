Scarab Beetle - Part of the reason scarab beetles were revered in ancient Egypt was because the Egyptian scarab beetle god, Kephara, used a car that looked very much like a beetle from a distance. It's an easy mistake to make. Scarab beetle amulets were often used by the Egyptians as adornments on mummies. This might be explained by the scarab beetle's famous habit of laying its eggs in dung heaps.
