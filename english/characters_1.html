Crash - Flash, brash and full of panache. This is one bandicoot who knows he's the leading light of his species. Don't be put off by Crash's lumbering ways, the blunder from downunder has the word 'funk' flowing through each one of his marsupial veins. Brought up in the Outback of Australia, Crash used to think that 'marsupial' was just a way of saying that his mother's cooking was ready. As his (now long and illustrious) career as a baddie beater took off, Crash was to pick up many little gems of information like this. Whilst he never really threatened to shine academically, Crash has been known to threaten academics with his yo-yo.

distinguishing marks: wild streak just under the chin
