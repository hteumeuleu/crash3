Scorpion - Scorpions are arachnids, which means it's perfectly alright to be petrified of them. They have eight legs (two of which have outsized claws attached) and a segmented tail housing the infamous sting. Brown in colour, they like warm places, and who can blame them? One rare species, the Ceilingoicles Hanguratus is said to lie in wait for its prey whilst suspending itself from the ceiling. Like all scorpions, this variety possesses a neurotoxin in its sting which has had the effect of making prey go drifting off into space. Creepy.
