Power-ups - Crash Bandicoot 3: Warped starts with the same move-set as at the end of Crash Bandicoot 2. When you beat Tiny Tiger (the first boss character), that set of moves has the super belly-flop added to it. With added power, this will allow Crash to splat more powerfully and with a greater radius than before. Every boss you beat will then give you not only access to a new area on the Warp Room but a new move. . .
