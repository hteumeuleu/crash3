Bandicoot - The two families of bandicoot species (Peramelidae and Thylacomyidae) live predominantly in Australia, Papua New Guinea, Indonesia and Tasmania; the legendary Perambulidae Thrideeus species is far more elusive and much harder to pin down to a particular continent. Being marsupials, none of the bandicoot species feels the need to wear a bum-bag.

Most bandicoots are omnivorous,
meaning that they're not fussy.
