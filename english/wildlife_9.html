Cobra (continued) - Coloured dark brown or black, cobras have ribs on either side of their heads which are covered by a loose flap of skin. These puff out when the cobra is disturbed by noisy tourists to form the archetypal angry-snake posture. The thing to remember here is that it's not just bravado and posturing; the cobra really means it. Follow the indian snake-charmers' example and stay just out of reach.
