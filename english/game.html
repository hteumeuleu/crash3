Warp Room - The Warp Room on Crash 3 plays in a similar way to previous Crash games. It's laid out on one floor, though, so you can see the five main areas of it even if you can't get to them yet. Each area of the Warp Room gives you access to five levels followed by a boss character. For classic Crash enthusiasts, Crash's moves on some of the levels will be familiar. In these levels he begins the game with the same set of moves that he ended Crash 2 with. But when the Bandicoots take to the roads, skies or seas, you can forget everything you learnt in previous Crash games.
