"About Time" - . . . said Cortex as his new ally landed on the planet hot-headedly. But Cortex already knew enough about time to know that he could go anywhere in it: forwards, backwards, past, future. . . he could even stretch his pants in five dimensions if he wanted to. Having teamed up with the fiendish Dr N. Brio, together the pair had built a contraption that could take Cortex and his underlings anywhere they wanted to go. . . today. And for Cortex, that meant ultimate power in all temporal dimensions. All he has to do to achieve it is collect the power-giving crystals hidden inconveniently in the mists of time and supreme rulership will be his. . .

. . . And all Crash has to do to stop him is collect the crystals before Cortex can take them through his vortex.
