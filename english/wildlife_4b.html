Shark (continued) - In addition, the shark can hear a pin drop underwater and even has electrical sensors that detect movement - basically, they know where the next meal is coming from.

Sharks are distinctive for their 'fusiform' body shape. This tapers and streamlines the body reducing drag through the water allowing maximum power to the fins. Scientists estimate that eating one bandicoot will result in 0.00000001% damage to a shark's total tooth enamel. This does not take into account the consequent tooth decay produced by failing to brush the teeth carefully after a meal. No empirical data exists for this.
