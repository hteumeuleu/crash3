How do you get an object to look realistic from a distance and finely detailed close up?
Your model is 200 polygons, 400 polygons, 600 polygons, 800 polygons depending on how close you get to it. The ships on the jet-ski levels are 1 600 polygons when you're close but it goes down to 200 from a distance. If you notice that, we haven't done it well because our goal was to make the game look as good as it always did without that stuff, but we needed to have it in to have the broad vistas in Crash 3.
