Jet-Ski - Coco's jet-ski is great for impressing beach bums with, but it has a much more useful application. Ride the waves avoiding bombs, rotating anchors, mad sailors in rowing boats and albatrosses as Coco jets her way between the buoys. The jet-ski has its own, dedicated engine and a unique set of controls which allow Coco to perform stunt flips and loops (for extra goodies) off the ramps.

Warning: shark-infested waters.
