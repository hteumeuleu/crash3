Tiger - The tiger is the largest member of the Cat family and is thought to have originated in the Pleistocene epoch of the Quaternary Period. Tigers traditionally live lonely existences and find it difficult to make friends. They would say that this has absolutely nothing to do with either the size of their claws or the sharpness of their teeth. Many of their enemies would disagree. Baby tigers have lovely, bouncy personalities, which is a pity, because in later life the probability of them turning into miserable loners is quite high.
Treat a tiger right when it's young though, and it'll do practically anything for you. . .
