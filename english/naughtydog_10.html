Have you got any tips for beating best times in Time Trial Mode?
When you're doing this kind of gameplay the jump with a flip is slower than a simple jump, spinning is slower than running - all these things are going to start to become important to you; you're going to start to learn these little tricks. Also, if you pass the box and spin, that gives you an extra third of a second, I believe. I'll give you an example of when this really becomes interesting. . .
