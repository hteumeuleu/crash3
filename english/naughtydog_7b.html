What does making Crash 3 Dual Shock compatible add to the game?
We've put Dual Shock features into Crash 3 everywhere where we thought it warranted it. When we first got the system we were, well, sceptical. But then we started playing around with it. . . And now we've had a lot of time to play with it - we're not sceptical anymore. It really works. When you go to the side of the road on the motorcycle round, when you're hitting the dirt and you're bouncing. . . you know it. You feel it.
