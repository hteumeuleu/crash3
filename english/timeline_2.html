But Crash wouldn't have got where he is today (right up there in the marsupial A-list) without some globe-threatening calamity befalling him; and this time it came in the form of a crash landing from outer space. No, not another Crash - there can be only one (of them). . . A strange, dark power suddenly entered Earth's atmosphere, landing somewhere in the ocean and filling everyone with dread. Only Uka-Uka knew the significance of this alien being, but to him it wasn't exactly alien.
