But surely making the water flow around all of the objects in it must have been tricky. . . ?
As you can see on the ramps, and on Coco as she rides the jet-ski, we've expanded the Z-buffer so that it is effectively as good as any hardware's going to give you. It's software driven but it's completely free-form - you can do what you want to do with it. Likewise, the bombs and the buoys are flat, which is a standard thing to do in these games. We don't allow the water to split the flat plane as a flat plane, instead (and you can see it well on the bombs) you get a curved cut around the flat plane, so you can preserve the round effect of the round shape.
