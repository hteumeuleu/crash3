Motorbike - Crash's motorbike has the technology of a powerful Dual Shock engine under the petrol tank; and this, coupled with one of the sportiest suspension systems ever invented, means that you feel every bump in the tarmac. If you happen to stray off the beaten track, you will also get slightly more than you bargained for. And for those occasions when extra speed is needed, there are turbo pads at strategic points on the highways that will give the bike a boost and accelerate it into a wheelie. The bike is harder to control on one wheel though, so you'll need to decide how fast you dare go before reverting to the more conservative two wheels.

Warning: there are unconfirmed reports of alien abductions on some of the Crash 3 roads.
