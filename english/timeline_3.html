Behind the Mask - Uka-Uka told Crash and Coco that things weren't right - his evil twin brother Aku-Aku had joined the world domination party, and that could only mean one thing. Aku-Aku was now in league with Crash's old adversary, Neo Cortex. Aku-Aku is a voodoo mask like his brother, but one committed to a life of skulduggery. His blackened, fiery face instils fear in all who meet it. . . Aku-Aku is just bruising for a rumble in the jungle.
