Triceratops (continued) - Literally meaning 'three horned face', many Triceratops have thought about plastic surgery, but the cost of such a large-scale operation is prohibitive to all but the richest of the species. This may explain reported cases of Triceratops going after other animals. . . the theory goes that the Triceratops will stop at nothing when it thinks there might be money involved.

Triceratops have so far not been found outside the Cretaceous period. The International Society of Plastic Surgeons is extremely grateful for this.
