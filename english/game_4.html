Relics - Picking up Relics from the past (or future) is a new gameplay mode in Crash 3. And it's this Time Trial mode that allows you to complete all the levels. . . completely. You are never prevented from going back into a level once you've completed it in Crash 3; and the reason is that when you do go back in, the first thing you'll see is a spinning clock. When you hit this, time will start ticking at the bottom of the screen, and the hunt for a Relic can begin. There are no longer any checkpoints, the Bandicoots won't die or lose any lives and question marks won't give you any fruit.
