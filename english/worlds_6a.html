Prehistoric - Crash's ancestral roots appear before his very eyes as he enters the lava flows and geezers of Earth's infancy. It's hot, the pterodactyls are restless and there's a big mumma triceratops that's out to get Crash's fur. And as if that wasn't enough, Crash comes face to face with one of his evolutionary precursors. . . it's not a pretty sight. On the plus side, he can keep his feet dry by hitching a ride on a baby T-Rex - completely cute till aged 4. . .
