Crash Bandicoot 3: Warped is a continuation of the story of the previous 2 Crash games. How important was it to get the balance right between continuity and providing players with new features and new gameplay?
Although we have new levels, we wanted to have at least one more major reason for players to re-visit the CD after they've completed it. So they can go through win on the first level by getting all the crystals; they can win on the second level, so to speak, by getting 100% on the game.
